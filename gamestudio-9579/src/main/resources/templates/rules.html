GAME RULES

1. Placing Phase

Players take turns placing their 9 pieces on any empty position. The goal is to form a "mill" — three pieces in a straight line.

2. Moving Phase

After all pieces are placed, players take turns moving their pieces to adjacent empty positions.

3. Flying Phase

When a player has only 3 pieces left, they may move to any vacant position on the board.

4. Mills

Forming a mill allows a player to remove one of the opponent's pieces (unless all are in mills).

5. Winning the Game

A player wins by reducing the opponent to fewer than 3 pieces or leaving them with no legal moves.
